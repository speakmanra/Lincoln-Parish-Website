  • Lincoln Parish

Lincoln Parish is a record producer, songwriter, and guitarist based in Nashville, best known as the lead guitarist for Cage the Elephant. Parish co-wrote and played guitar on albums Cage the Elephant (Relentless), Thank You Happy Birthday (Jive), and 2015 GRAMMY Award Nominee for Best Alternative Album Melophobia (RCA), including the No. 1 single “Come A Little Closer”. Parish left the band in 2013 to build his Nashville recording studio Talk Box Rodeo where he currently produces, writes and develops artists full-time.

The control room at my studio is equipped with a API 1608 console, as well as 14 external pre amps, and 4 compressors. The main live room has a full drumset, baby grand piano, and 16 available microphone inputs. There is access to a large collection of microphones, guitars, keyboards, and amplifiers.
